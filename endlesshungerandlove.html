Endless Hunger and Eternal Love
--------------------------------

I have tasted you in whispers,
in stolen words, in the spaces between sighs,
but it is never enough—
you are never enough, because I could drown
a thousand times and still thirst for more.

I crave you, not just in body,
but in the way your soul pulls mine closer,
in the way your absence leaves me aching,
in the way I am never whole without you.

Every night, the longing grows,
a fire that neither time nor distance can dim.
But I do not want us to burn in secrecy—
I want us to rise, to make this love whole,
to make it something the heavens bless.

Let this hunger be more than just desire.
Let it be the hunger to build a life,
to wake beside you, not in fleeting moments,
but in the eternity of what is ours to claim.

I do not want to love you in halves,
in longing glances and restless nights.
I want to love you in daylight, in permanence,
in the name of something greater than us.

Because no matter how much I have of you,
I will always want more—

~The Desperate One
