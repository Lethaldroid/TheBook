/**
Waiting Till the End
**/

It has been so many days,
so many nights where the moon dims,
so many mornings where the sun rises
but never reaches me.

You were here once
real, warm, breathing against me,
your touch filling the emptiness.

Yet the world, cruel and unyielding,
tore you away,
bound you in chains of fear and control,
hands that should have held you,
but only suffocated.

Why must kindness suffer?
Why must the one who gave me life
bear such weight?

I reach for you in the dark,
but grasp only absence.
Yet if love could shatter walls,
I would tear through every restraint
to bring you home.

But you endur.e
a caged bird who knows the sky,
a wildflower crushed yet unbroken,
a flame bending in the wind,
never extinguished.

Love does not break.
It waits. It survives.

And so I wait.
with every breath, every heartbeat,
holding you in the quiet spaces
where no one can take you from me.

And when the day comes,
when your chains are dust,
I will be here.
loving you, waiting for you, always.

Yours truly,
despite the distance.

--- END OF POEM ---
