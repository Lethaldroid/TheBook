*********
THE FALL
*********
I fell for someone
For Someone, I Shouldn't have.

Its a Sin, I know
But what's a sin if never
comitted?

But then what is love
If it is never confessed?

Does God hate us or
tells us that there are things
that are never meant to be?

I'm going to hell...
For I fell
For someone
For someone, I shouldn't have.

~The one who fell
