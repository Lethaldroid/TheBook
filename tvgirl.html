*********
In the Moonlit Nights
*********

You may as well take my heart, love,
it beats your name anyway.
What use is a lock without the key
when you've already made a home inside me?

We’ll trade rings not just in metal,
but in vow, in vein, in soul.
Not to cage or claim,
but to whisper: we are bound
not in chains, but in choice.

You, still finding your shape
in this strange, wide world
I will not hurry your bloom.
I’ll walk beside you
barefoot through every question,
patient as spring waiting for the thaw.

And when you will find moonlit nights
strangely empty, and you call my name into the quiet,
I swear
you’ll never find silence.
There will always be a voice,
mine tender, steady, certain,
rising like breath against your ear:
"I'm here."

Even in the stillest shadows,
I’ll be the echo your doubt can’t silence,
the weightless warmth beside you,
the rhythm beneath your rest.

I love you
not like a flame, destined to vanish,
but like gravity,
quiet, constant, inescapable.

More than my convictions,
more than what I once thought was unshakable,
you shook me awake,
and I loved you more than the rules
I wrote for myself before you.

I love you
more than anything that came before.
I do.
And I always will.

~Bound in your love
