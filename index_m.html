--------------------------------
THE BLACK ROSE
--------------------------------
Though the black roses
Were embroidered on your
Darkened dress, yet somehow they
Bloomed in my heart.

What in the world
Did you make me feel?

An eternal dusk,
A garden of shadows,
A whisper of petals,
A bouquet of moonlight,
A lost teardrop on velvet,
A word unspoken.

Though the black roses
Were just embroidered on your
Darkened dress.
~Your Sunflower
