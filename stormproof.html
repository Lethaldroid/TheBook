Stormproof
--------------------------------

You think we break
every time we bend.
That cracks mean collapse
but love isn't porcelain.
Itâ€™s weathered wood,
seasoned by storms,
still holding up the roof
we built with our names.

You try to fix the thunder.
Smooth the lightning.
Make the sky apologize
for being a sky.
But listen
I never asked for forever clear.
Just for you
to be my shelter
when it rains.

We forget the third-last fights
because it was just
a ripple in the river.
Not the flood.
Not the end.
And what I remember
is how you made me laugh
the day after
how you danced
in the debris.

Do I love you
and go fight someone else?
Hell no.
You're my chaos
and my calm.
My apology
and my anthem.
The war and the white flag.

Yin and yang,
storm and still.
We burn.
We rebuild.
And every time,
we come home.

To each other.

~Your Lighthouse
